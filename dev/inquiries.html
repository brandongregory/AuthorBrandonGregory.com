Article Inquiries

I’m open to writing for just about any publication that would welcome my work or expertise. Just a few things to be aware of:

  1. If there’s a woman or person of color equally qualified, ask them first.
  2. If you have no women or people of color in your list of authors, really think about getting one before approaching me. Seriously.
  3. I typically get paid for my articles. That’s not a hard rule, but it greatly increases your chances of me accepting.

Still interested? Contact me and maybe we can work something out!

Mentorship/Feedback Requests

Maybe you’re just looking for someone to read over something you wrote or bounce a few ideas off of. Maybe you want to get started in writing but don’t know where to start. No problem—let’s chat. Contact me and let me know what you need help with.
